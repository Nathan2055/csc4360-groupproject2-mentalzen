PowerPoint Slide Show Guidelines:

User:

Clearly state the problem and outline general requirements including inputs and outputs.

Design:

Provide an overview of the solution, highlighting key design features, user interface, UML class diagrams, and pseudo code. Utilize Transforms, Transitions, and Animation for enhanced presentation.

Testing (if applicable):

Explain the testing process, including the test plan, data used, and bug tracking/reporting.

Group Team Name:

Assign a team name related to Mobile Application Dev Project.

Presenter(s):

It is mandatory for all team members to participate in the presentation. Options for presenting include: Allowing each team member to present their individual work. All parties must actively engage in the presentation process.

Incorporate Code Snippets:

Utilize PowerPoint to present, ensuring to insert code snippets within slides.

Video Hosting Guidelines:

  • Title Video: Name it following the format "Task Name_Team Name".
  • Duration: Aim for a video length of 20-25 minutes
  • Participation: All team members must contribute to the video.
  • Hosting Options:
    • YouTube: Establish a YouTube channel with the group name and upload your video
    • Google Drive: Upload your video to a shared Google Drive folder with appropriate sharing permissions
  • Recording Presentation Software: Choose from options such as Zoom, Microsoft Teams, WebEx, or Discord.
  • Upload Process: Once prepared, upload the video to your chosen platform for future reference and submissions.
